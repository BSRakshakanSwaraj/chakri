About BGMI

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by Krafton. It is a rebranded version of PUBG Mobile tailored for the Indian audience.

Features

  • Stunning graphics and immersive gameplay
  • Various modes including Classic, Arcade, and more
  • Regular updates and new content
  • Exclusive in-game events and rewards
